How to Use

🎤 Microphone Mode

Use your phone as a PC microphone.

  1. Open PC app & click Start Listening.
  2. Enter PC's IP address on phone app.
  3. Tap Start Microphone on phone.

🔊 Speaker Mode

Use your phone as a PC speaker.

  1. Tap Start Speaker on phone app.
  2. Enter PC's IP address on PC app speaker tab.
  3. Click Start Streaming on PC.
